How to Apply?

STEP 01

Apply Through JSKILLS

Submit your application via the official Registration Form on this page.

STEP 02

Review & Acceptance

JSKILLS Project Team reviews your application within 3 days. Qualifying candidates receive a Letter of Acceptance.

STEP 03

Financing Application

Use your Letter of Acceptance at Peneraju.org. Ensure you CTC Birth Certificate, NRIC, and Bank Statement.

STEP 04

Approval within 30 Days

Receive an Invoice from JSKILLS. Complete your EFSA with ALTI LO and upload all within 7 days.

STEP 05

Receive Funds

Funds disbursed to your Bank Account within 20 days. Proceed to pay back to JSKILLS to finalize registration.

STEP 06

Start Your Training

Begin your certification journey with no financial stress and full professional support.

Eligibility Criteria

Malaysian Bumiputera.
Active individual bank account under applicant’s name.
Diploma qualification with minimum 1 year related experience or equivalent.
Be able to commit fully to the program duration.

Investment & Structure

Program Fee: RM3,500.00 (Inclusive of Exam & 8% SST).
Duration: 5 Days (Fully Online).
Financing: Easy Payment Plan (EPP) and YP ALTI support available.
Who: Professionals, Diploma/Degree holders, and Business leaders.

Learning Outcomes

Design structured and effective prompts for diverse business scenarios to maximize accuracy.
Optimize AI responses for clarity and accuracy to enhance strategic productivity.
Integrate AI tools and insights into operational decision-making and business simulations.
